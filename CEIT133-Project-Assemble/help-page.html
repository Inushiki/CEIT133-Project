What are the principles of securely trading on Debookers?
Debookers' number one priority is always the safety and satisfaction of our users. We want you to have a good experience when trading at Debookers. Before you start looking for a new laptop or advertise for your unused bike, we recommend reading the following suggestions:
-There are millions of attractive deals on Debookers, but you should be careful with advertisements that have unrealistic prices, look like they were taken from the catalog, or are clearly not photographed by the user. If an offer sounds too good to be true, it probably is.
- Always check the seller's or buyer's profile page.
- If you have doubts about the integrity of the user, find another seller / buyer. There are many more users on Debookers!
- The best way to complete a purchase is to make delivery and payment at the same time. You should never choose to pay in advance by money order or credit card.
- Make sure that matters such as information about the status of the product, price and payment method are clearly stated and agreed before the meeting.
- Before making the payment, please examine the product and check if everything matches the description of the seller.
- Never give out personal financial information to buy or sell.
- Please don't accept bank checks and money orders.
- If you have been exposed to a possible fraud on our site or a user who harassed you, please notify us of this user so that we can investigate the issue and take the necessary action.
- The vast majority of Debookers users are highly reliable people. In addition, posting or shopping online is a method that is widely used safely in Turkey and around the world. However, when buying and selling things on the internet, necessary precautions must be taken.

Be careful when someone contacts you and tells you to reach them by email or text and send you a check. This is a common scam method. Please report this user to us, block him, and opt for other buyers using letgo chat who are considering paying cash so that we can take action.
